How to Play

  • Players alternate turns placing a stone of their color on an empty intersection (using the mouse)
  • Winner is the first player to form an unbroken chain of five stones horizontally, vertically, or diagonally
  • Placing so that a line of more than five stones of the same color is created does not result in a win
  • Before starting, select either a 15x15 or 19x19 grid. Black plays first. Good Luck!
